Introduction

Hello!

I'm Mert, an Amsterdam-based Digital Project Manager with over 10 years of experience working across global agency networks like Monks, OLIVER Agency, Publicis Groupe, and Havas CX. I've had the privilege of collaborating with incredible brands such as Diageo (Johnnie Walker), Nike, Sanofi, Mercedes-Benz, Peugeot, Philips, and Netflix.

Throughout my career, I've always enjoyed bridging the gap between creatives and brands, ensuring ideas are not only brought to life but also exceed expectations.

I recently launched my freelance company, Flow-Creative, which focuses on helping businesses streamline their processes and create work that truly resonates. I'm eager to partner with new clients and bring fresh ideas.

Let's work together to make something great!
